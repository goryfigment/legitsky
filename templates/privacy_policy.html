Privacy Policy

Rise Up Gamers does not share, nor will it share any of your information. We do not have an account registry to avoid collecting any consumer information and avoid giving our users any troubles.

Website:


Your search history and favorite items will be cached and as such stored on your browser. This will help reduce the need of useless pop-ups and other unnecessary information being thrown at you. In addition to this, if you delete the cache on your browser all this data will be deleted and the next time you visit this website it will be as if you had never visited our site before. This applies not only to our page but is a general practice which applies to any other site. If you have any questions please feel free to email us at whey2ezenterprises@gmail.com
